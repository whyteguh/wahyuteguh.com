## About this tool

The classic life-balance wheel, drag-to-score instead of a form.

- **Interactive wheel** — drag any dot in/out along its spoke to set that area's score from 1–10.
- **Live dashboard** — average score, highest area, lowest area, updated as you drag.
- **Focus recommendation** — a message pointing at your lowest-scoring area, so you know what to work on first.
- **Area reflections** — click a dot to write notes on why that area scored the way it did.
- **Reset** — clear all scores and notes back to a fresh wheel.

Saved in your browser only — nothing is sent anywhere.
