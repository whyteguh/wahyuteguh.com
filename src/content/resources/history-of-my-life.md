## About this tool

A quiet, once-a-year ritual: sit down and audit a year of your life.

- Give the year a **theme**, then jot the **highs**, **lows**, and the one **lesson** it taught you.
- Add short notes on **career**, **social**, and **health**, and rate your overall satisfaction.
- Build a **timeline** — add past years one at a time and revisit them any time.
- Switch to the **summary** view to see every year at a glance.
- Hit **Analisis AI** to copy a ready-made prompt of your whole history — paste it into Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini for patterns and strategic advice.

Everything is saved in your browser only — nothing is sent anywhere. Available in Bahasa Indonesia.
