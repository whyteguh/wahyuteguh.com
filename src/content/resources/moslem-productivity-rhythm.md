## About this tool

A daily rhythm planner built on a simple idea: the productivity science of
ultradian cycles and circadian energy fits naturally with how the sunnah
already structures a day.

- **Day starts at Maghrib** — the Islamic day begins at sunset, and so does your timeline (with both Gregorian and Hijri dates).
- **Ultradian cycles** — free time between anchors is filled with 90-minute deep-focus blocks and 20-minute breaks.
- **Circadian awareness** — each block is labeled with your likely energy level (peak, trough, rebound) so you match the task to the hour.
- **Sunnah anchors** — the five prayers, dhuha, qailulah before Dhuhr, and qiyamullail frame the schedule; a guide panel explains each.
- **Prayer times by location** — one tap fetches accurate times for where you are (with manual override).
- Write a target for every focus block, close the day with a reflection, and export it all to your clipboard.

Settings, notes, and reflections are saved in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere. Available in Bahasa Indonesia and English.
