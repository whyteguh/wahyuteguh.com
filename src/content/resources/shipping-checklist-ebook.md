## What's inside

- The **pre-launch checklist** — 27 checkpoints covering copy, performance,
  SEO basics, and the stuff everyone forgets (favicons, OG images, 404 pages).
- **Launch-day playbook** — where to post, in what order, and why timing
  matters less than you think.
- **Post-launch triage** — how to read your first week of feedback without
  overreacting to it.

## Who it's for

Anyone who builds and ships on the web — indie hackers, freelancers, or
developers launching their first side project.
