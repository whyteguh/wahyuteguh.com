## About this tool

A drag-and-drop Eisenhower matrix for sorting tasks by urgency and importance.

- **Canvas view** — drag task nodes anywhere on a zoomable board; position alone decides the quadrant.
- **List view** — the same tasks grouped into Do First / Schedule / Delegate / Don't Do, drag between columns.
- **Node details** — title, description, and an accent color per task, editable from the side panel.
- **Zoom & center** — zoom in/out and snap back to center on the canvas.

Saved in your browser only — nothing is sent anywhere.
