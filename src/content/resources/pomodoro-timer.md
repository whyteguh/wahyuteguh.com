## About this tool

Two timers in one, and nothing else on the screen:

- **Pomodoro** — the classic: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes of break, on repeat.
- **Flowmodoro** — no fixed blocks. Focus as long as you're in flow; when you take a break, you earn one fifth of your focus time.

It also keeps simple statistics for the day — total focus time, sessions,
average focus length, and your focus : break ratio — stored only in your
browser.

- Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere
- Works offline once loaded
- Keyboard friendly: <kbd>Space</kbd> to start / take a break, <kbd>S</kbd> to stop
