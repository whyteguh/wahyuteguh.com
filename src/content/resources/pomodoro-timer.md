## About this tool

This is the pomodoro timer I actually use: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes
of break, and nothing else on the screen.

- Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is sent anywhere
- Works offline once loaded
- Keyboard friendly: press <kbd>Space</kbd> to start/pause

If it helps you, share it with a friend.
